## Mouse

```

Button:
- 0 Any
- 1 Left Click
- 2 Right Click
- 3 Middle Click
- 4 Up
- 5 Down

Context:
- R RootWindow
- I Icon
- F Frame Corners
- S Frame Sides
- T Title Bar
- W Window
- 13579 08642

Key Modifiers
- A Any
- C Control
- S Shift
- M Meta (Alt)
- N Nothing
- 1-5 mod1-mod5
```
